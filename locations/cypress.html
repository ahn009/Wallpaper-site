FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How to remove wallpaper?

Wallpaper removal involves scoring the surface, applying steam or removal solution, and scraping carefully. Many homeowners prefer professional removal services to avoid wall damage. Proper removal is crucial for preparing walls for new wallpaper or paint.

What is peel and stick wallpaper?

Peel and stick wallpaper is self-adhesive backing that doesn't require paste. It's popular for rental properties and DIY projects. This removable wallpaper is ideal for temporary decor changes and can be easily installed without professional help.

Where to buy wallpaper?

Wallpaper can be purchased at home improvement stores, specialty wallpaper shops, and online retailers. Many local suppliers offer professional consultation and installation services to help you choose the perfect wallpaper for your project.

How to prepare walls for wallpaper?

Wall preparation involves cleaning, repairing cracks, and applying primer. Proper preparation ensures wallpaper adhesion and long-lasting results. Smooth, dry walls are essential for successful wallpaper installation in any home.

What is the best wallpaper for bathrooms?

For bathrooms, moisture-resistant vinyl wallpaper is recommended. It withstands humidity and is easy to clean. Many homeowners choose waterproof options specifically designed for high-moisture areas like bathrooms.
